Here at RockCCTV  - We only install Digital IP cameras - as we consider installing an analogue system today would not give our customers the desired level of future proofing that we as a business would want to provide. POE ( Power over Ethernet ) gives unrivalled clarity to your recordings.

We locate your recorder box in your loft - recording all activity for at least a rolling three weeks. Should anything occur in that time you can always be assured that it will have been recorded and retained. The recorder is best protected from the dusty environment of your loft by being housed in a beautiful wooden box. We've even thought of pine cones glued to the inside to keep the spiders at bay.

Our prices are very competitive. Send us some photos of your house ( or business ) and after a few questions about your home - We'll probably be able to give you a quote over the phone.

Typically we recommend a 4 camera Sannce system :-

     1 * 4CH 1080P NVR - 1 Terrabyte SSD Recorder
     4 * 1080P Day / Night POE IP Networked Cameras
     1 * 25mtr Camera Cable plus
     3 * 15mtr Camera Cables
     1 * CD + User Manual + Warning Sticker
     1 * Ethernet extender pair to connect to your router
         Powered from the lighting circuit in your loft.

Our setup includes configuring your phone to access your cameras.

12 month warranty on all parts and our labour.

What we provide

  • Camera Systems

    Sannce and Annke systems have built in motion detection and can identify movement across the field of view, marking the recordings with movement a different colour.

    Although we prefer Sannce and Annke systems, we are also able to install other camera systems - please contact us for more information.

    The wooden box has a hole cut in the bottom. Only when the temperature of one of the chips inside the NVR exceeds 45°C does a filtered fan kick in to add additional cooling. This stops unnecessary dirt being brought into the box.

  • Camera Location Survey

    We provide a Camera Location Survey prior to installing your camera system - although sending us photographs of your desired locations in advance is always helpful. During our survey we can show you a live feed from one of our customers - who has very kindly agreed to us sharing his feed. This shows the clarity of the video from the cameras that is being recorded. Depending on the location of your property you might be restricted as to what you can record within the field of your cameras - schools are of course of particular relevance.

  • Telephone Support


    We offer telephone support on a ring back basis - best time to catch us is the evenings till 9pm or Saturdays. Please avoid phoning outside these times and on Sundays. Don't forget you can email us at any time.

  • Dynamic Upgrade paths


    We always keep abreast of developments and can always recommend the best solution for you.

  • Annual Service contracts


    We offer our customers an Annual Service contract - which includes monitoring your installation, cleaning the camera lens, and making sure that everything is working to your satisfaction.

  • If you live in semi-detached house - Have you considered sharing ?


    Share the camera system and the installation cost with your neighbour.
    When you set up your control box access account - you give your neighbour access to the account - and then they can watch - and remote view all four cameras. This benefits both neighbours - as it halves the price for both of you, but most importantly it gives better coverage than two independent two camera systems. It also means that people can't sneak up your neighbours path and then get access to the front of your house out of sight of your cameras.

    Whether or not you get your neighbours on board - Our prices are very competitive
